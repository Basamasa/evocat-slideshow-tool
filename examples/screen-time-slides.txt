Your Screen Time is high. Now what?

Check your top app first

Don't fix the whole phone
Pick the app you reopen automatically.

Protect your worst time
Bedtime, mornings, work, or study.

Start with one clean hour
Not a perfect day.

Move the app out of reach
Less visible means less automatic.

The goal is not zero phone use
The goal is fewer automatic openings.
