What to do based on your Screen Time number

Under 2 hours/day
Keep your phone plain. Do not add new distractions.

3 hours/day
Protect it. Don't let short videos become your default break.

4 hours/day
Move the worst app off your home screen. Make it search-only.

5 hours/day
Turn off badges. Red numbers pull you back.

6 hours/day
Add friction before the app opens. Make the habit visible.

7+ hours/day
Delete the shortcut. Put one pause between the urge and the tap.
